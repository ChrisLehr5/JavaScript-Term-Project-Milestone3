Purpose:
This game is a text based adventure game. 
You type in the commands to move your character around and interact with the world. 
Choose your options wisely, as some options have dire consequences!
There is a help menu and interface commands that display what your character current inventory. 

Issues:
The game is pretty short and somewhat stilted. It needs to be expanded quite abit, and the npc characters need to be fleshed out. 

Future Releases:
Expanding the world and the characters.
