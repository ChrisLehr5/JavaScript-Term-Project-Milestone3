Purpose:
This game is a Where's Waldo search game.
You use the mouse as a magnifying glass to search the image
for the listed items. 

Issues:
Only one page to search, and the mouse has no click abilities. You have
to manually fill out the checkboxes.  

Future Releases:
Adding more to search for, and on Onclick event.
