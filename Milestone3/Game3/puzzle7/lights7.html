The history of lights out:

This 5 x 5 grid game was created in 1995 on a handheld by Tiger electronics. A 3 x 3 grid was created in the 1970's - it relies on mathmatics to solve the light chasing method.

If a light is on, it must be toggled an odd number of times to be turned off. If a light is off, it must be toggled an even number of times (including none at all) for it to remain off. Several conclusions are used for the game's strategy.

How to play:

Click on the light square to light the cube up, and click again to turn the cube off.

When all the lights are in the "on" position - You've won!
